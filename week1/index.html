Mobile First User Experience

Mobile design first helps to create a simple user experience for apps. There is a huge range of devices that we need to design for, not just the personal computer anymore. Screen real estate issues and touch interaction issues are major problems when not designing mobile first. Designing mobile first gives a much better experience across all devices and helps address issues such as touch interaction and screen real estate. Mobile first ensures you focus on content and functionality, "start small and scale up". Starting with the core functionality and working up helps make things easier to scale.

Be there, Be Useful, Be Quick, Make them not even notice. Put important things in easy to reach places. Reduce image usage, use SVG/web fonts, optimize and minify files, gzip to eliminate unnecessary data, Many users have no idea what the hamburger menu is. Having the menu at the bottom provides a better user experience. Mobile ads should not be taking up large parts of the screen real estate. It's important to think of simple creative ways to interest users with your ad.
